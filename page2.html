Volcano and mount hiking preparation

Hiking in Rwanda is a great way to push yourself and get some exercise while visiting. Rwanda's many hills offer hiking experiences for all skill levels, from novices to experts. For the basic, beginner-level hikes, you will be good to go without any specialized hiking equipment. You will be good to go without any specialized hiking.

All you need is:

A nice pair of sports shoes with good gripbr

A bottle of water

Sunscreen (even when you think you don not need it. The weather may change while you are on the hike)

Some snacks to eat on the way

Your phone or camera to take some snaps
